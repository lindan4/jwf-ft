Services Offered

Chicken

Our chicken is renowned for its exceptional quality. We offer air-chilled, grain-fed chicken that is raised locally in Ontario without the use of hormones or antibiotics. This ensures a healthier and more natural product that retains its moisture and flavor.

Pork

Areas of Operation

J & W Foods Inc. operates primarily within Ontario, offering both retail and wholesale services from our Scarborough headquarters.

  • Retail Butcher Shop
  • Wholesale Distribution
  • Online

Our retail butcher shop, located in Scarborough, serves as a one-stop-shop for health-conscious consumers. Here, customers can find a wide range of premium graded meats, including chicken, beef, pork, and seafood, all sourced from trusted local farmers and suppliers.

Our online platform extends our reach by allowing customers to order products conveniently from their homes. The online ordering system, particularly for our Meal Butler service, simplifies the process of purchasing fully cooked, portion-controlled meals. We deliver across Ontario, ensuring that our healthy and nutritious meal options are accessible to a wider audience.

We serve numerous restaurants, catering services, and other food businesses throughout Ontario. Our wholesale operations are designed to meet the needs of businesses seeking high-quality meats and food products at competitive prices. We pride ourselves on maintaining strong relationships with our clients, ensuring timely and efficient deliveries.

Overview

J & W Foods Inc. was co-founded in 1995 by Stavros Trougakos and William Fehr, inspired by their father, George Trougakos, a passionate butcher and grocer in downtown Toronto since the early 1960s. Building on George's legacy and entrepreneurial spirit, J & W Foods has grown into a renowned distributor and butcher shop, known for its commitment to providing high-quality, natural meats and nutritious foods.

Mission and Vision

Our mission is to offer premium, natural meats and health-focused food products that support our customers' wellness goals. We envision a community where high-quality, nutritious foods are accessible to all, fostering a healthier lifestyle.
